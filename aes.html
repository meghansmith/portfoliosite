Atlanta Exec Suites

A New Site Design & Develop Project

Scope: Create a website for a new Executive Suites office building.This project's keywords included Prestige, Professional, and Friendly. MSS worked with Atlanta Exec Suites to write the copy - tailoring it to the type of small business owners they're seeking to bring in. If you will need to make frequent changes to your site, MSS will build out a Content Management System for you. Since the information on this site would be updated rarely, we went with a Static website. Even with a static web site, you can still have forms and sign-ups for email lists.

The main purpose of this site is to get people to schedule appointments or request quotes. Those calls-to-action are prominently displayed beneath each major section of the homepage. The photos and lists of benefits show off the features that office-seekers are interested in. A clean background, high-impact fonts, and beautiful images were essential to the feel of this project.

Like all the sites we create, this one displays beautifully on all sized devices. Customers who are looking from their cell phones, tablets, and computers all get an excellent experience.

Additional work included:

  • Branding and Logo Design
  • Form Development
  • SEO Work
  • Google My Places Business Listing

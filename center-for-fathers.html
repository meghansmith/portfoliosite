Center for Fathers

A Student Design & Develop Project

Scope: Redesign the Center for Fathers & Families website - primary focus on UX/UI and IA. This project had two main audiences that both needed to be addressed: fathers seeking help and potential donors. I redesigned the home page with columns to address the different users. The information for fathers is prominently displayed and clearly explained. In designing the navigation, I completely re-worked their site map to better reflect their users' needs. I also completed a donate form and a template for their internal pages.

On the donate form, I wrote all the content. The suggested donation amounts and descriptions were aimed to tie in emotions and utilize the principle of anchoring. I also designed a banner for the top of that page to include a more human touch.

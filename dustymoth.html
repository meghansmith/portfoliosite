Dusty Moth Brewing Compay

Phase One of a Design and Development Project

Scope for Phase 1: Create a website that will generate interest establish branding.Dusty Moth Brewing Company is looking to open in the South Plains of West Texas in 2019. They wanted a site that would establish their branding and story as they build relationships with suppliers. The site needed to be flexible, as it will need to represent their business as it grows.

Getting just the right images and feel for this project was very important. The brewery wants to be distinctly 'West Texas' and family-friendly. The simple layout and hand-drawn artwork help establish that. Fonts, color schemes, and wide-open spaces also work toward those goals. To keep a modern edge, we adjusted how the images move when the page scrolls - check out the animated gif below.

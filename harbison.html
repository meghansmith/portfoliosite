Harbison Community Association

A Redesign and WordPress Development Site

Scope: Redesign a very large, constantly changing websiteHarbison needed an updated website with a new look and an easy to use way to keep it current. Their old Content Management System was cumbersome and difficult to use, and as a result, the site didn't have up-tp-date information on their programs and offerings.

Harbison has a lot they offer the community. The site needs to stay updated and easy to navigate for two main types of people: those interested in finding out what's going on at the Community Center and those who are interested in the Associations rules, policies, and management. We thoroughly went over every page in their old site to organize them in a way that makes sense to their audience.

The Content Management System used is WordPress. By taking advantage of the exceptional customization available, we were able to create ways for the HCA employees to easily update the site. In minutes they can update the news, add programs and have them show under multiple categories, and re-order how they appear on the page.

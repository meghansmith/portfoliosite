About Me

I think work is fun!

I’m a web developer with a love of code. I enjoy crafting beautiful solutions. As a detailed and dedicated person, I use great communication and design to turn ideas into reality. I’ve discovered a passion for design and code that connects websites and their audiences. Complex problem solving is something I thrive on, so it’s wonderful to use those skills every day in this industry. I have experience in researching, content creation and curation, UX/UI design, project management, and coding. The projects featured here, including this site were designed and coded in 8 weeks as I went through The Iron Yard’s Web Design Academy.

I was born and raised in Green Bay, WI, but now live in Columbia, SC. Some of my favorite things have always been writing, drawing, math, and puzzles. It’s a joy to be able to combine all of these in one career! Whether you need someone to design and code or to turn mock-ups into websites, reach out to me. Let's create something great together!

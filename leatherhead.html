Leatherhead Brewing Company

A design & develop project in Wordpress

Scope: Design and Build a custom website for a craft brewery The goal of this project was to let potential customers know what's unique and special about Leatherhead Brewing Company. Since their beers, events, and specials change frequently, this site was made in Wordpress. This allows them to easily add and adjust their own content while maintaining the look and feel we rolled out initially.

They wanted their beers list up to date. From their taphunter feed, I was able to add styles to the beer list - it updates automatically when they change their taps with the vendor, but the colors and fonts fit in with the look and feel of the website.

There's a blog to keep customers up to date. We created an email sign-up and template for their e-newsletters. All they have to do is update the headlines, text, and images and it's ready to go with their branding.

Murphy & Grantland

A Student Design & Develop Project

Scope: Create a solid corporate site. Determine calls to action, find balance between UX/UI and specific “client” requests.The project brief stated that they wanted a simple corporate site that had some personality and texture. I chose the blue of their logo, a contrasting burgundy, and a light paper texture for the background to imply trustworthiness and respectability. The header images were chosen to make a big statement representative of their core values.

I created the practice areas page, attorney directory, and an attorney detail template. I rotated through the values in their statement in the overlay. By choosing a business card format for the attorney directory, I put large photos and all the essential details in expected positions. This makes it easy for the user to skim and locate their desired information.

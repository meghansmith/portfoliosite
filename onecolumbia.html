One Columbia

A Student Design & Develop Project

Scope: Create a fun, easy to navigate site that clearly displays event information. Focus on research and UX/UI.The only design directive was to keep the circle from the logo. Starting with that circle, I designed the feel for the website. I took the opportunity to learn how to use pseudo elements to create tape and tacks in CSS. I used a wood background and added shadows to the elements to increase the feel of posters on a fence.

My research determined that most users would want to find events by either category or date, so I made both options readily available. I organized the information so that the most important items would be on top, and established typographical hierarchy to make it easy to scan.

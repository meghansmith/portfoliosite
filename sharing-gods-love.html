Sharing God's Love

A design & develop project in Wordpress

Scope: Create a website with two main audiences: those in the community needing help and those looking to volunteer or donate.This is an awesome local charity supported by more than 100 area churches. They help hundreds of families each week and needed a website as wonderful as they are.

Their current needs and events were often changing. Since they're on multiple areas of the website, I used a format that allows them to update just once, and they'll be updated everywhere they appear.

The home page is full of images, with specific calls to those in crisis and those who are looking to help. The internal pages for "Get Help" and "Get Involved" have more specifics, but we kept lots of faces and images there to balance the text and support the friendliness of Sharing God's Love.
